Frequently Asked Questions

How does the escrow process protect me as a buyer?

As a buyer, your funds are held securely until you receive and approve the goods or services. This prevents you from losing your money to fraudulent sellers. If the seller fails to deliver, the funds are returned to you.

What happens in case of a dispute?

Our dispute resolution team will review all transaction details and communications. We work with both parties to reach a fair resolution, and the funds are held until the dispute is settled.

How long does it take for funds to be released?

Once the buyer accepts the delivery, funds are typically released to the seller's account within 1-2 business days, depending on the payment method used.
